The Bible is the inspired word of God. It is living and active, sharper than a double-edged sword (Hebrews 4:12).

### Key Learnings:
1. **Inspiration:** How we know the Bible is God's word.
2. **Context:** Rules of interpretation (Hermeneutics).
3. **Application:** Moving from information to transformation.

**Practical Activation:**
Choose one verse from Psalms this week and meditate on it every day, writing down different gleanings each time.
