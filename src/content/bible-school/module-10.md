The family is the bedrock of society and the church. God's design for the family brings order, peace, and blessing.

### Key Learnings:
1. **Marriage:** The covenant between a man and a woman as a picture of Christ and the Church.
2. **Parenting:** Raising children in the discipline and instruction of the Lord.
3. **Singleness:** Honoring God in all seasons of life.

**Practical Activation:**
Write a note or send a text of appreciation to your spouse, a family member, or a spiritual mentor today.
