Worship is more than a song; it is a posture of the heart that recognizes the worthiness of God.

### Key Learnings:
1. **Spirit and Truth:** The kind of worshippers the Father seeks (John 4).
2. **The Sacrifice of Praise:** Praising God even when circumstances are difficult.
3. **The Power of Music:** How worship shifts spiritual atmospheres.

**Practical Activation:**
Designate a 15-minute window today to put on worship music and sing your own praises to God, regardless of how your day is going.
