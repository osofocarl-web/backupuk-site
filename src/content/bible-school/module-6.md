We are all called to be a witness. Evangelism is simply sharing the good news of what Jesus has done for you and for the world.

### Key Learnings:
1. **The Mandate:** Understanding the Great Commission (Matthew 28).
2. **Your Story:** Formulating your personal testimony.
3. **Compassion:** Seeing people through the eyes of Jesus.

**Practical Activation:**
Write out your 3-minute testimony (Before Christ, How you met Christ, After Christ) and practice sharing it with someone.
