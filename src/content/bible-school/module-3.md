Prayer is the breath of the believer. It is a two-way communication: speaking to God and listening for His voice.

### Key Learnings:
1. **The Lord's Prayer:** A model for comprehensive prayer.
2. **Intercession:** Standing in the gap for others.
3. **Fasting:** Combining prayer with fasting for spiritual breakthrough.

**Practical Activation:**
Set an alarm for 12:00 PM every day this week to stop and pray for 5 minutes for your family and church leaders.
