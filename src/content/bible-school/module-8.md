Money is a tool, not a master. How we manage what God has entrusted to us is a test of our stewardship.

### Key Learnings:
1. **The Owner:** Recognizing that God owns everything, we are just managers.
2. **Tithing & Offerings:** The principle of the firstfruits.
3. **Debt & Integrity:** Approaching finances with biblical wisdom.

**Practical Activation:**
Review your budget this week. Are your finances aligned with what you value spiritually? Set a goal to give to a new cause this month.
