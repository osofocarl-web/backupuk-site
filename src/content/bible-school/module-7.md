Faith is the substance of things hoped for, the evidence of things not seen (Hebrews 11:1). Healing is part of the atonement provided by Christ.

### Key Learnings:
1. **The Source of Sickness:** Understanding that Jesus came to destroy the works of the devil.
2. **Divine Healing:** The biblical precedent and promises for healing.
3. **Activating Faith:** How to pray and activate faith for healing.

**Practical Activation:**
Is there someone in your life who is sick? Pray for them today. If possible, ask if you can pray for them directly.
