We are in a spiritual battle, but the war has already been won by Jesus. Our job is to enforce the victory.

### Key Learnings:
1. **The Enemy:** Understanding who Satan is and his tactics.
2. **The Armor:** Putting on the full armor of God (Ephesians 6).
3. **Authority:** Using the name of Jesus and the Word of God as our weapons.

**Practical Activation:**
Read Ephesians 6:10-18 out loud today, verbally declaring that you are putting on each piece of the armor of God.
