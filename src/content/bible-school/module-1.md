The foundation of the Christian life is the understanding of what happened at the cross. Salvation is not just a one-time event, but the beginning of a living relationship with God.

### Key Learnings:
1. **Grace vs Works:** Understanding that we are saved by grace through faith.
2. **New Creation:** Recognizing your new identity in Christ (2 Corinthians 5:17).
3. **Daily Walk:** The importance of prayer and the Word in sustaining spiritual life.

**Practical Activation:**
Write down three things that changed in your perspective since you started your walk with God. Sharing your testimony is the first step in evangelism.
