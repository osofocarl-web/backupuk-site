True leadership in the Kingdom of God is servanthood. Discipleship is teaching others to obey what Jesus commanded.

### Key Learnings:
1. **Servant Leadership:** Following the example of Jesus washing the disciples' feet.
2. **Mentorship:** The Paul and Timothy dynamic of passing the baton.
3. **Multiplication:** Moving from being a disciple to making disciples.

**Practical Activation:**
Identify one person in your life you can intentionally encourage and pour into spiritually over the next three months.
