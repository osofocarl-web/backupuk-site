The Church is not a building, but the people. We are called to fellowship, encourage one another, and build each other up.

### Key Learnings:
1. **The Body:** Understanding spiritual anatomy (1 Corinthians 12).
2. **Commitment:** Why regular attendance and participation matters (Hebrews 10:25).
3. **Serving:** Finding your place to serve within the local body.

**Practical Activation:**
Reach out to two people in your local church this week just to encourage them and see how they are doing.
