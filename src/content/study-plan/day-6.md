God gave the Law to Moses not to bind the people, but to show them how to live as a holy nation set apart for His purposes. The Law acted as a mirror, revealing humanity's inability to perfectly obey.

### Foundations
The Law shows us God's holy standard. While we could never keep it perfectly, Jesus did. Now, under the New Covenant, the Spirit empowers us to fulfill the righteous requirements of the Law through love.

**Life Application:**
Examine your heart regarding the first commandment. Is there anything taking the place of God as the primary priority in your life?
