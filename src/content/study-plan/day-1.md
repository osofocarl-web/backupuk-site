Welcome to Day 1 of your journey through the Word!

Today we look at the very beginning of everything. In Genesis, we see the power of God's word bringing order out of chaos. In John, we see that Jesus (the Word) was there from the start.

### Foundations
Understanding God as Creator is the first step in any biblical education. It establishes His authority, His love, and His creative purpose for your life.

**Life Application:**
Take a moment today to look at something in nature and remember that the same God who designed the stars designed you with a purpose.
