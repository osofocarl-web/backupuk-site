The Israelites went from honored guests in Egypt to brutally oppressed slaves. Yet, in the midst of this darkness, God was raising up a deliverer. 

### Foundations
God's plans cannot be thwarted by any human king. When Pharoah tried to destroy the Israelites, God used Pharoah's own daughter to raise the one who would lead them to freedom.

**Life Application:**
God often uses the very things that seem like our biggest obstacles to become the training ground for our greatest calling. Trust His process today.
