Today we look at the reality of sin. The first humans chose to disobey God, bringing a curse upon humanity and separating us from our Creator. But even in Genesis 3, there is a hint of the Savior to come.

### Foundations
Understanding sin is crucial because it points to our desperate need for a Savior. If we don't understand the depth of our brokenness, we can't truly appreciate the height of God's grace.

**Life Application:**
When you face temptation today, remember that Jesus has broken the power of sin. You have a choice to walk in obedience!
