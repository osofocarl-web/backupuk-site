God calls Abram out of his pagan surroundings to begin a new nation through which the Messiah will eventually come. It wasn't Abram's perfection that pleased God, but his faith.

### Foundations
God always works through covenants. Understanding the Abrahamic covenant helps us understand the overarching redemptive plan of God. We are grafted into this promise by faith.

**Life Application:**
God may be calling you to step out in faith into unfamiliar territory. Trust that His promises are true and His leading is good.
