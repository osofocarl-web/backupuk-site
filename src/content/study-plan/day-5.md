The final plague upon Egypt was the death of the firstborn. The only way to escape was to apply the blood of an unblemished lamb to the doorposts of the house. When the judgment came, it would "pass over" those under the blood.

### Foundations
The entire Old Testament sacrificial system points directly to Jesus. We are saved not by our good works, but because God sees the blood of Jesus applied to our lives.

**Life Application:**
Take communion in your home today, remembering the incredible price Jesus paid to rescue you from the consequences of sin.
