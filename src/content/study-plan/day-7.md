While Moses was on the mountain receiving the Law, the people below were breaking it. They formed a golden calf, declaring it the god who brought them out of Egypt.

### Foundations
Idolatry is not just bowing down to statues; it is anything that takes the place of God as the ultimate source of security and satisfaction in your life.

**Life Application:**
What "golden calves" do you tend to turn to when you feel like God is delaying His promises? Surrender them today.
